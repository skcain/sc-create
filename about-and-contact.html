About my Process

When I work on a project, I’m driven to continue learning throughout every stage of the process. I begin with independent research and competitive analysis to better understand the context around the problem.

Next, and most importantly, I listen to the people who already use or potentially will engage with the system. By building relationships, I can expand upon existing expertise and uncover perspectives that impact my own approach.

Communicating those learnings to my team helps us collaboratively brainstorm needs, assumptions and tests that lead to user-centered solutions.
